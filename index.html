About ME

My Story

My background in coding started when I was young. I would come home from school every day and work on a project that I had in mind and would spend almost the whole night doing it.

For me, the sheer ability to change the lives of potentially millions of people around the world, just by writing code in my room every night on my old Pentium computer got me excited beyond words.

I have always loved what I do. Since then, I have co-founded a Startup and founded my own Company when I was 19.

I have worked with companies, developers, and freelancers in and outside of India, which has helped me learn about the problems that companies and people from different industries face. I learned how to put myself in the client's shoes, try to understand their problems and work towards solving them.

I would love to work towards solving problems that are faced by an industry or a group of people, and would like to tackle challenges while doing so.

Would you like to work with me? Feel free to reach out!
